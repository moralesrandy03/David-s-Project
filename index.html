David's Adventures

The adventures of David in New York city. Surviving day by day in this concrete jungle. Most of the time he is trying to find new things to do and new people to connect with. David is always willing to help others within his possibilities. Lover of pilsener beer and fun meetings. He goes around the city looking for new experiences. he always try to find the positive things between bad situations and he helps solve this situations. After helping others and solving problems, he believes fun times are deserved.
